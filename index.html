Who We Are

A UICT Team Building Real-World Tech

We are Group 5 — SmartTech, a team of five IT for Business students at the Uganda Institute of Information and Communications Technology (UICT), Kampala. Our coursework spans software development, web design, database management, and AI — skills we brought together to build DigiRise, our group final-year project.

Our group final-year project, DigiRise, is a live AI-powered platform that lets Ugandan small businesses build professional websites in minutes — no technical skills needed. It's deployed and live at digirise-final.vercel.app.
